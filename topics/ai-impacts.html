AI Impacts All Levels of the Library

Large Language Models (LLMs) are really the first wide-spread generalist artificial intelligence. As mentioned in a recent report by Antropic's economists, Libraries (and higher education) have a higher theoretical AI coverage and as a result have a higher observed AI coverage.

Patrons, Librarians, and Library Staff likely use generative AI in a variety ways. For example:

  • Patrons: Ask reference librarians for assistance in finding sources mentioned in chat sessions with LLMs
  • Administrators: LLMs assist in analysis and writing reports to library stakeholders
  • Catalogers: Use LLMs to summarize electronic text, assist in Subject Headings Assignment
  • Circulation: LLMs assist in patron communication via email or chat
