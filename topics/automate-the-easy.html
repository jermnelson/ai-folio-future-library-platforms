Impact of error ↓ (Low ↓ High) Low volume High volume
Low impact Optional / Nice-to-have
  • Automate if convenient
  • Low payoff
 Automate first
  • Rules-based

High impact Keep manual / Expert-led
  • Rare but consequential
  • Requires judgment
 Automate with guardrails + QA

"Easy" Candidates

  • Deduping Inventory Records on ISBN,DOI or other fields
  • Adding/Correcting Call Numbers
  • Holds, Overdue, and other Routine Notices
  • E-resource Troubleshooting

Checklist for Tasks

  • Reversible? Can we roll back changes easily?
  • Detectable? Will errors be obvious (validation rules, reports)?
  • Contained? Does the task affect a single record vs many systems/patrons?
  • Permissions? Does the task touch restricted data (patron info, licensed content)?
  • Audit? Are there logs that provide "who/what changed" visibility?
